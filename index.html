Why FlowBot?

Quick Deployment

Set up in under 3 minutes – no technical skills required. Just connect your knowledge base and go live.

Document Integration

Supports Notion, PDFs, FAQs, and more. Just upload your docs and FlowBot becomes your 24/7 knowledge expert.

Embeddable Widget

Easily integrate FlowBot into your website with one line of code or use as a standalone page for instant access.

Multilingual Support

Serve your customers in Arabic, French, Spanish, English, and more languages — with zero extra work.

Customizable Tone

Set your bot's personality to friendly, formal, or technical without complex prompt engineering.

How FlowBot Works

1

Upload Your Knowledge

Import your help docs, PDFs, Notion pages, or any structured FAQ content. FlowBot reads and organizes it into a knowledge base.

2

FlowBot Learns Automatically

Once your content is uploaded, FlowBot fine-tunes itself — no prompts, no flow builders. It understands tone, context, and structure.

3

Deploy Anywhere

Share FlowBot via a hosted link or embed it directly on your site — perfect for onboarding pages, support portals, and landing pages.

4

Improve with Feedback

See which questions are asked most, what's working, and where your documentation could be improved.

Founder Story

I'm Hassan, and I built FlowBot out of necessity. I've always loved optimizing systems and removing bottlenecks — especially in small teams. After working with a fast-moving team where support questions constantly interrupted our flow, I realized much of the knowledge we needed was already documented — just hidden or underused. FlowBot gives that knowledge a voice. It helps teams scale support without scaling overhead — fast, simple, and beautifully effective.

Hassan Alsahli Founder, FlowBot

Who It's For

Handling Repetitive Questions

Stop answering the same questions over and over. FlowBot helps you reclaim hours each week by automatically responding to common inquiries.

Streamlining Onboarding

Make it easier for new team members and clients to get up to speed. FlowBot puts your entire knowledge base at their fingertips, available 24/7.

Growing teams need to do more with less. FlowBot helps you scale your support operations without expanding headcount or stretching existing resources.
