Why FlowBot?

Built for Lean Teams

FlowBot is purpose-built for solo founders and 2–3 person teams. No bloated dashboards, no complex setup.

Paste a Doc, Go Live

Upload a PDF, paste a help doc, or link to a Notion page — your AI support agent goes live in minutes. No code needed.

Easy to Embed

Add FlowBot to any site or share a hosted link. Perfect even if you don't have a website.

Multilingual from Day One

Serve your customers in Arabic, French, Spanish, or English — with zero extra work.

Customize Tone

Customize your bot's tone — formal, friendly, or technical — without learning prompt engineering.

Founder Story

I created FlowBot because I believe support should be smart, fast, and invisible. As a founder, I've wasted hours answering the same questions or digging through docs. So I built the tool I wished I had — one that learns from your content and gives instant answers to customers or new team members.

FlowBot is for makers, solopreneurs, and lean teams. It's your AI teammate that never sleeps and always knows where to find the answer.

Sarah Chen Founder, FlowBot
