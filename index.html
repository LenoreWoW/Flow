Founder Story

FlowBot was born out of frustration. As a founder working with distributed teams across different time zones, I found myself constantly fielding the same questions about our product, documentation, and processes.

When team members needed information at 3 AM my time, they had to either wait hours for a response or dig through scattered documents to find answers. This slowed down our momentum and created bottlenecks.

I realized this is a common problem for teams of all sizes - information gets siloed, knowledge is scattered across different tools, and both team members and customers waste valuable time searching for answers.

That's why I built FlowBot - to create a single source of truth that learns from your existing documentation and provides instant answers 24/7, regardless of time zones or team availability.

Hassan Alsahli Founder, FlowBot
